Recent Projects

"Uncle Jim and the Black Crows"

time lapse video

This mural is painted in the Sideways Lounge located in Buellton, California. The central figure in the mural was inspired from the history of the Santa Ynez Valley and features an old vaquero, nicknamed 'Uncle Jim'. He is flanked on both sides by the Black Crows,who have their own mural and exist elsewhere throughout the property. Check out the time lapse video of its creation.

Grand Lake Lodge -project

Grand Lake, CO

Image Gallery

Grand Lakes Lodge is a nationally recognized historical building, located in the Rocky Mountain National Park, in Grand Lake, CO. Our work there included; painting five murals, the curation of a permanent display of historical photographs, design support, color consulting and overseeing the clean up, repair and installation of a large collection of taxidermy.

"What a Wonderful World"

time lapse video

Created for the Sideways Inn located in Buellton, California. The mural celebrates the music of Louis Armstrong and his song titled "What a Wonderful World." Check out the timelapse video of its creation.
